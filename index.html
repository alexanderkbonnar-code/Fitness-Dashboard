Quick Weekly Dashboard

Male Programme

Goal: fat loss, muscle gain, better body composition, better VO2 max, improved 5k and aerobic fitness.

Male Week 1

Monday - Strength A

Session completed

Squat 4 x 8, RDL 4 x 10, Floor Press 4 x 10, 1-arm Row 4 x 12/side, RFESS 3 x 10/leg, Suitcase March 3 x 45 sec/side, Core 3 rounds

Tuesday - VO2 Intervals

Session completed

6 x 1 min hard / 2 min easy

Thursday - Strength B

Session completed

Deadlift/RDL 4 x 8, OHP 4 x 8, Inverted Row 4 x 10, Goblet Squat 3 x 10, Push-up 3 x 12, Arms, Hollow Hold

Friday - Zone 2

Session completed

35 min conversational run or cycle

Saturday - Strength C

Session completed

Bulgarian Split Squat 4 x 8/leg, Hip Thrust 4 x 12, Row 3 x 12, DB Press Drop Set, Lateral Raise, Rope Finisher, Plank

Male Weeks 2-12 Progression

Week 2: add 1-2 reps where possible, VO2 7 x 1 min hard / 2 min easy, Z2 40 min.

Week 3: push top sets to RPE 8, VO2 8 x 1 min hard / 90 sec easy, Z2 45 min.

Week 4: deload, halve sets, VO2 5 x 1 min hard / 2 min easy, Z2 30-35 min.

Week 5: compounds shift toward 6-8 or 8-10 rep ranges, VO2 5 x 2 min hard / 2 min easy, Z2 45 min.

Week 6: 6 x 2 min hard / 2 min easy, Z2 50 min.

Week 7: 6 x 2 min hard / 90 sec easy, Z2 50 min.

Week 8: deload, 4 x 2 min hard / 2 min easy, Z2 35-40 min.

Week 9: 4 x 3 min hard / 2 min easy, Z2 50 min.

Week 10: 5 x 3 min hard / 2 min easy, Z2 55 min.

Week 11: 5 x 3 min hard / 90 sec easy, Z2 60 min.

Week 12: deload + benchmark, 3 x 3 min hard / 2 min easy, optional 5k test.

Female Programme

Goal: fat loss, strength, muscle gain, major glute emphasis, trail-friendly conditioning.

Female Week 1

Monday - Strength A

Session completed

Back Squat 4 x 8, Hip Thrust 5 x 10, Bulgarian Split Squat 3 x 10/leg, DB RDL 3 x 12, Glute Med Work, Side Plank

Tuesday - Strength B + Rope

Session completed

Glute Bridge 4 x 12, SL RDL 3 x 10/leg, Step-up 3 x 10/leg, Frog Pumps 3 x 20, Donkey Kicks, Rope 6 x 30 sec on / 60 sec off

Thursday - Strength C

Session completed

OHP 4 x 10, 1-arm Row 4 x 12/side, Goblet Squat 3 x 10, Floor Press 3 x 10, Reverse Lunge 3 x 10/leg, Lateral Raise, Dead Bug

Friday - Trail Conditioning

Session completed

30 min brisk trail walk

Saturday - Strength D

Session completed

RDL 4 x 10, Hip Thrust Mechanical Set, FFESS 3 x 10/leg, Push-up, Row, Glute Burnout Circuit

Female Weeks 2-12 Progression

Week 2: add 1-2 reps where possible, trail 35 min brisk walk + 4 x 30 sec uphill pushes.

Week 3: optional extra hip thrust set if recovery is good, rope 8 x 30 sec on / 45 sec off, trail 40 min walk + hill pushes.

Week 4: deload, halve sets, rope 4-5 easy rounds, trail 30 min easy walk.

Week 5: run/walk begins, 1 min jog / 3 min walk x 8, rope 8 x 40/40.

Week 6: 90 sec jog / 3 min walk.

Week 7: 2 min jog / 3 min walk.

Week 8: deload week, easy trail walk only.

Week 9: 3 min jog / 2 min walk, rope 8 x 45/30.

Week 10: 4 min jog / 2 min walk.

Week 11: 5 min jog / 2 min walk, rope 10 x 45/30.

Week 12: deload + benchmark trail session.
